About

A cosy little pizza spot

Valentino’s is a small, no-nonsense takeaway with wooden counters, friendly faces and a menu built around proper comfort food.

Locally run, made with care

We’re a neighbourhood takeaway through and through. The kitchen is small, the ovens are hot and everything that leaves the counter is cooked to order. Pop in, say hello and grab your favourite pizza, kebab, pasta, burger or chips on the way home.

Space Compact shop, designed for quick collection and takeaway.
Seating No tables, just wooden benches and counters while you wait.
Vibe Casual, laid-back and open late – ideal for a chilled night in.
Price Main dishes usually sit between £10 and £20.

What we serve

Classic pizzas with your favourite toppings, generous kebabs, hearty pasta dishes, burgers and proper chip-shop style chips. Mix and match for the perfect takeaway spread.
